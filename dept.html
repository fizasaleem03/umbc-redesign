Program Overview

The IS program provides students with strong technical, analytical, and communication skills. Courses cover systems analysis, database design, networking, and business integration.

Key Courses Include:

  • IS 300: Management Information Systems
  • IS 310: Software and Hardware Concepts
  • IS 410: Database Application Development
  • IS 450: Network Design and Management
  • IS 303: Human-Computer Interaction
